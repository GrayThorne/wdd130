Overview

Purpose

I want to make a leather crafting website to sell products and raw materials for other to enjoy the craft. As well as have a community forum for the crafters to teach, learn and just talk to one another. Do you want to learn leather crafting, or be able to buy and enjoy others work? Having one place to do buy/sell and teach/learn as well as get the materials needed is what I want to provide.

Audience

This site is for anyone who is interested in working with leather. I want the experienced crafters to be able and willing to teach new crafters who want to learn. Being able to have a place for the beginner and expert to talk and buy supplies. Having a place to talk to others is a big part of what we do for everyone.

Content

Home page

Welcome to Rock Leather, if you are here, you must have an interest in either leather products or leather crafting itself. We have both a place to buy and order custom work or premade leather goods as well as a wonderful leather crafter community. Enjoy the wonders of leather and the beauty it brings to our lives and others. Leather crafting is a wonderful craft to make practical and beautiful pieces of art. The leather goods that are crafted can last a lifetime and the skills can be passed to new crafters that wish to learn.

Our community leather page is a great place to talk to other crafters and get their advice and help or just talk about the fun of working with leather. It is a great place to find tons of information. Our merchandise page is for those that would like to get leather supplies and goods. We offer lots of options and would love to help your love of crafting or your start to crafting.
