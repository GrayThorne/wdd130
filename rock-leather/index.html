Welcome to Rock Leather

If you are here, you must have an interest in either leather products or leather crafting itself. We have both a place to buy and order custom work or premade leather goods as well as a wonderful leather crafter community.

Enjoy the wonders of leather and the beauty it brings to our lives and others. Leather crafting is a wonderful craft to make practical and beautiful pieces of art. The leather goods that are crafted can last a lifetime and the skills can be passed to new crafters that wish to learn.

Our Community

The community leather page is a great place to talk to other crafters and get their advice and help or just talk about the fun of working with leather. It is a great place to find tons of information. Our merchandise page is for those that would like to get leather supplies and goods. We offer lots of options and would love to help your love of crafting or your start to crafting.
